About Us

Dedicated to building inclusive, thriving housing communities

Our Mission

At AI Advantage Solutions, we're committed to supporting housing providers across Ontario who are creating mixed income communities and delivering near market rental housing. We believe that everyone deserves access to quality, affordable housing in communities where they can thrive.

Our work centers on helping housing providers deliver exceptional value to their tenants while maintaining operational excellence and full AODA compliance.

What We Do

We partner with housing providers, community housing corporations, non-profits, and municipal housing authorities to:

  • Develop Mixed Income Communities: Strategic planning and implementation support for creating economically diverse, socially integrated housing communities
  • Optimize Near Market Rental Programs: Expertise in program design, tenant selection, rent-geared-to-income calculations, and sustainable operations
  • Ensure AODA Compliance: Full accessibility compliance for all communications, digital platforms, and tenant services
  • Enhance Tenant Experience: Tools and strategies to improve tenant engagement, satisfaction, and community building
  • Streamline Operations: Process optimization, automation, and efficiency improvements that save time and resources

Why AODA Compliance Matters

Accessibility isn't just about compliance – it's about ensuring every tenant, regardless of ability, can fully access your services and participate in their community. With approximately 1 in 4 Ontarians living with a disability, AODA compliance is essential to serving all members of your community.

We build accessibility into everything we do, from keyboard navigation and screen reader compatibility to clear communication and inclusive design.

Our Approach

We understand that housing providers face unique challenges – balancing affordability with sustainability, meeting diverse tenant needs, navigating complex regulations, and building strong communities. Our solutions are:

  • Practical: Designed for real-world implementation, not just theory
  • Tenant-Centered: Focused on improving experiences for the people you serve
  • Compliant: Meeting or exceeding AODA and other regulatory requirements
  • Sustainable: Built for long-term success and scalability
  • Value-Driven: Delivering measurable improvements to your operations and community outcomes

Based in Hamilton, Serving Ontario

Located in Hamilton, Ontario, we're proud to serve housing providers across the province. We understand the Ontario housing landscape, regulatory environment, and the communities you serve.
