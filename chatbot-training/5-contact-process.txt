WHAT TO PREPARE FOR YOUR CONSULTATION

To make the most of your consultation, consider having ready:

For AODA Compliance:
- Current website URL
- List of digital platforms you use
- Any accessibility concerns you've identified
- Documents/forms you currently use with tenants

For Mixed Income Communities:
- Current or planned income mix
- Number of units
- Target demographics
- Key challenges you're facing

For Near Market Rentals:
- Current rent structure
- Income verification process
- Waitlist management approach
- Subsidy programs involved

For Process Optimization:
- Current processes that feel inefficient
- Bottlenecks or pain points
- Systems currently in use
- Staff time allocation issues

For Tenant Engagement:
- Current communication methods
- Tenant satisfaction data (if available)
- Challenges with tenant engagement
- Community events or programs

For Digital Solutions:
- Website goals
- Target audience
- Features needed (portals, applications, etc.)
- Current technology stack
