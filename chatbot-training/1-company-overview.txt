Who We Serve:
We serve housing providers across Ontario, including:
- Community housing corporations

- Municipal housing authorities
- Near market rental providers

Our Mission:
At AI Advantage Solutions, we're committed to supporting housing providers across Ontario who are creating mixed income communities and delivering near market rental housing. We believe that everyone deserves access to quality, affordable housing in communities where they can thrive.

Our work centers on helping housing providers deliver exceptional value to their tenants while maintaining operational excellence and full AODA compliance.

What We Do:
We partner with housing providers to:

1. Develop Mixed Income Communities
   - Strategic planning and implementation support for creating economically diverse, socially integrated housing communities

2. Optimize Near Market Rental Programs
   - Expertise in program design, tenant selection, rent-geared-to-income calculations, and sustainable operations

3. Ensure AODA Compliance
   - Full accessibility compliance for all communications, digital platforms, and tenant services

4. Enhance Tenant Experience
   - Tools and strategies to improve tenant engagement, satisfaction, and community building

5. Streamline Operations
   - Process optimization, automation, and efficiency improvements that save time and resources
